Directions

  1. Smash the bananas and mix in the milk.
  2. Whisk in the protein powder and baking powder, mix in the oats.
  3. Warm the blueberries mixed in the maple syrup in the microwave for 30 seconds, then evenly space the blueberries on the top of the oat mixture.
  4. Drop the vanilla yogurt onto the oat mixture randomly, use a chopstick or similar to swirl the yogurt around in the oat mixture.
  5. Bake at 350Â°F for 40 minutes.
