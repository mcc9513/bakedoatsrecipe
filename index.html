Why Baked Oats Make the Best Breakfast

First, the combination of nutritious oats and antioxidant-rich blueberries, plus protein packed greek yogurt, provide a powerful boost of energy to start the day.


Secondly, the convenience of meal prep is unbeatable. Spending a little time on the weekend to prepare a batch ensures that I have healthy and delicious breakfasts ready to eat throughout the week.


Lastly, the aroma of warm oats and the burst of sweetness from the blueberries and greek yogurt make every morning feel like a treat. Blueberry Baked Oats have become a morning ritual for me that sets a positive tone for the day ahead.
